Road to the PGA Tour

My Story

Bombastic rhythm and acoustic blues, Nathan Strubhart, armed with soulful grooves and piercing lyrical ammo all fired off with a wink and a shot of whisky. The tunesmith seesaws from deft songwriting craftsman in the studio to effortless showman on the stage. His tempo and key changes keep you guessing, while the melodic prose will keep you hooked...
